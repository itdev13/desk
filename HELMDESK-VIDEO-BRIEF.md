# HelmDesk — Animated Video Brief

Paste this whole file into an AI video generator (Sora, Runway, Pika, Kling, Veo, or a script-to-video tool) as context. It describes the product, the look, the scenes to animate, and a ready-to-use script. Aim: a **30–60 second animated promo/explainer**.

---

## 1. What HelmDesk is (the elevator pitch)

**HelmDesk is a white-label helpdesk & ticketing app.** It turns every inbound customer message — from SMS, email, WhatsApp, live chat, social, and more — into a tracked support **ticket** with SLAs, a Kanban board, agent routing, and a reporting dashboard. Agencies install it, brand it as their own, and resell it to their clients.

**One-liner:** *"Every customer message becomes a tracked ticket — with SLAs, routing, and a clean queue your team actually enjoys."*

**Tagline options:** *"Stay at the helm of support."* · *"Every message, a tracked ticket."*

**Metaphor:** *Helm* = a ship's steering wheel → calm command of a busy support operation. Lean into "in control / at the helm / calm seas" — but keep it modern SaaS, **not** nautical-kitsch (no anchors, ropes, pirates).

**Audience:** marketing agencies and their small-business clients. Tone: confident, calm, professional but warm.

## 2. Visual identity (use these exactly)

**Palette**
- **Amber (hero accent):** `#E0A24A` — buttons, highlights, logo tile, active states
- **Amber dark:** `#B97E2C` · **Amber soft wash:** `#FBEEDC`
- **Ink (near-black, navy-charcoal — NOT pure black):** `#0F1729`
- **Paper (cool off-white bg):** `#F5F7FA` · **Panel/cards:** `#FFFFFF`
- **Status colors** (only for ticket state, never decoration): green `#2F9E6B` (in-SLA / good), amber `#D9822B` (approaching), red `#D64545` (breached/overdue), blue `#3B6FB0` (info)

**Logo:** a bold **"H" monogram** in a rounded-square **amber tile**, "H" in white/ink. Clean, geometric, crisp.

**Typography:** modern sans-serif (Inter / Geist / SF feel), semi-bold headings with tight tracking; a monospace face for small data (ticket refs `HD-42`, SLA timers, plan labels).

**Aesthetic ("slate-console"):** rounded corners (~10–14px), soft shadows, generous whitespace, calm and organized — the opposite of a cluttered inbox. Priority shown as a colored left **stripe** on ticket rows; status as small **pills/chips**.

**Motion feel:** smooth, confident, unhurried. Elements slide/fade in with gentle easing. One orchestrated "hero moment" beats scattered effects. Avoid frantic or overly-flashy animation.

**Hard don'ts:** ❌ never show the words **"GoHighLevel", "HighLevel", or "GHL"** (white-label product). ❌ no pure black. ❌ no purple/blue "AI-startup" gradients. ❌ no nautical clip-art.

## 3. The screens to animate (real product surfaces)

Animate these as clean, modern UI mockups (amber-on-ink/paper):

1. **Inbox (hero screen)** — a 3-pane workspace: ticket **list** on the left (rows with colored priority stripes, `HD-11`, subject, contact, live green→amber→red SLA countdown), a **conversation thread** in the center (customer messages + agent replies as chat bubbles, a reply box), and a **details** panel on the right (contact card, priority/assignee/status, SLA).
2. **Kanban board** — columns *New / Open / Pending / Resolved*, draggable ticket cards with priority stripes.
3. **Dashboard** — KPI stat cards (Open, Overdue, Avg first reply, In-SLA %) + a created-vs-resolved trend line chart.
4. **A message → ticket transformation** — the signature moment: a raw incoming chat/SMS/email bubble visually *morphs* into a structured ticket card (`HD-12`, priority pill, SLA timer starts ticking).
5. **SLA countdown** — a timer on a ticket ticking down, color shifting green → amber → red (a breach turning a ticket red and surfacing under "Overdue").
6. **Setup wizard** — a friendly 4-step onboarding (Channels → Filters → Assignment → SLA) with an amber progress bar. (Optional, for an "easy setup" beat.)

## 4. Suggested 45-second scene script

**Scene 1 — The problem (0–6s)**
Chaotic scattered message bubbles (SMS, email, WhatsApp, chat icons) floating in disarray on a cool paper background. Text overlay: *"Customer messages everywhere."*

**Scene 2 — The transformation (6–14s)**
The scattered bubbles get pulled in and **morph into neat ticket cards** dropping into an organized list (each with an amber priority stripe + `HD-##` ref). An SLA timer starts ticking on the top card. Overlay: *"HelmDesk turns them into tracked tickets."* Amber "H" logo animates in.

**Scene 3 — The Inbox in action (14–26s)**
Glide into the 3-pane Inbox. A ticket is clicked → the conversation thread slides in center → an agent types a reply → hits **Send** (amber button) → the ticket status pill flips to *Resolved* (green). Overlay: *"Reply, assign, and resolve — all in one place."*

**Scene 4 — Control & insight (26–36s)**
Quick, smooth cuts: the **Kanban board** (a card drags New → Resolved) → the **Dashboard** (KPI numbers count up, the In-SLA % hits a satisfying green, the trend line draws itself). Overlay: *"SLAs, routing, and reporting — built in."*

**Scene 5 — White-label + close (36–45s)**
The HelmDesk "H" logo tile briefly **re-skins** to a generic agency brand color (showing white-label) then back. End card: the amber **"H" logo** centered on ink/paper with the tagline *"Stay at the helm of support."* and a soft "Get HelmDesk" call-to-action.
